#STAYHOME

If you have cough or have high temperature, do not leave your home for 7 days from when your symptoms started.

This will help protect others around you while you are infectious.

LEARN MORE

Plan ahead and ask others for help to ensure that you can successfully stay at home.

Ask your employer, friends and family to help you get the things you need to stay at home.

Try to stay at least 3 steps away from other people in your home.

LEARN MORE

Wash your hands regularly for 20 seconds. Use soap, or hand sanitiser.

Stay away from the elderly and those with underlying health conditions, as much as possible.
